Milford Sound, located in the southwest of New Zealand's South Island, is a stunning fjord renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty. Carved by glaciers during the Ice Age, this majestic destination is surrounded by towering cliffs, cascading waterfalls, and lush rainforests. Visitors can experience its dramatic scenery through boat cruises, kayaking, and scenic flights. Home to diverse wildlife, including dolphins, seals, and penguins, Milford Sound offers an unforgettable connection with nature.
